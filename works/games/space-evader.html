Game Development: "Space Evader"

Space Evader is an individual activity for the subject Introduction to Computing.


When: 2023

Method: Scratch

Role: Author


The goal was to make a sprite fly. I thought that most of the students will do the most basic thing- and since I already got experience with Construct 3, why not experiment with the instructions and make a rocket? It also "flies". Aside from flight, the sprite should also be able to collect points so I did- by using mini stars instead of coins. The game should also have clouds, well there are no clouds in space so I used rocks that will reduce the ship's health if both collided. Also, The sprite should be able to change color so I thought of changing its color to red whenever it collides with a rock. Easy.
